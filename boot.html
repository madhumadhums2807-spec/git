Welcome to Our Institute

Empowering students with world-class education and innovation.

"SMVIT" stands for the Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology, a private engineering college in Bengaluru, Karnataka. Established in 1986, it is named after the notable Indian engineer and statesman, Sir Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya. Key Details Location: The institute is situated on a sprawling 133-acre campus near the Air Force station in Yelahanka, close to the Bengaluru International Airport.
